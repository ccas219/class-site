This is a website sketch based on My Website is a Shifting House by Laurel Schwulst. The concept behind the sketch is a "neighborhood" that grows based on the number of people visiting. After a certain number of visitors, new houses are built and sold to anyone who wants to own a home. Home-owners are able to open their homes to visitors at any time for any occasion.


This is a website sketch based on Hello, World! by Taeyoon Choi. Choi talked about how computers use binary to work, and humans do as well. However, as humans we also have the in-betweens. Choi explained how "light and dark" is the human version of a computer's "0 and 1", but humans also have things like "dawn and dusk" which work as in-betweens. The idea behind this website sketch telling the time through binary and color. The example on the site simply says "The time is 5:00 p.m.", but you wouldn't knwo that unless you looked it up. However, you could assume it was sunrise or sunset by the color of the sky in the background.
